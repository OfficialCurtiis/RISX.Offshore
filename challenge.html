Rules

Challenge Structure

  • One run. No top-ups. Hit the goal or your run ends.
  • Start credits, goal, and limits are defined by the selected tier.
  • Max bet (Mines & Crash): 15% of balance • Max bet (Plinko): 10% of balance.
  • Mines cashouts capped at 4.5x. Plinko can hit up to 40x (tier-dependent).

Restart

  • If you fail, a restart window may be available at a discounted price.
  • When the timer expires, the standard restart price applies.

Payments & Confirmations

  • Payments are on-chain and may require confirmations before access is granted.
  • If paid but not unlocked, use your Payment ID in Support.

Fair Play

  • Outcomes are generated deterministically from seeded logic (provably fair-style).
  • Abuse, automation, or exploit attempts may result in access being revoked.
Fair Play

RISX uses seeded, deterministic logic for outcome generation. Results are not influenced by wallet balance or user identity.

If something looks off, contact Support with your session details and (if applicable) Payment ID.
